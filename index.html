About us

Welcome to Reliance Matric.Hr.Sec.School

"Rome wasn't built a day"

Similarly, "Reliance wasn't built under the bowers of academicians rather than reliable trustees schoolie".

First and foremost, Reliance Matriculation Higher Secondary School is one of the best known co-educational institution from KG to XII aims to give a new lease of life to rural students for high education.

Learn More

MISSION


Our school mission is to inspire continual achievement by fostering, safe, giving free hand with positive and nurturing environment, where learning teaching, diversity and equality are valued. We develop the students skill - questioning and learning.

VISION


Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them to send over backwards as lifelong learners and to contribute rural to the global world.
